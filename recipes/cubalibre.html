Cuba Libre

A classic Cuba libre recipe made with rum, cola, and a splash of lime

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Total Time: 5 mins
  • Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 4 ice cubes
  • 2 fluid ounces rum
  • 4 fluid ounces cola
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice, or to taste

Directions

Fill a 12-ounce glass with ice cubes. Pour rum and cola into the glass. Stir in lime juice and serve.
