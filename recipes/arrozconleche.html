Arroz Con Leche

This easy recipe will teach you how to make arroz con leche

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Total Time: 50 mins

Servings: 6

Arroz con Leche Ingredients:

These are the ingredients you’ll need for this homemade arroz con leche recipe:

  • Water: Cook the rice in two cups of water.
  • Cinnamon: Cook the rice with cinnamon sticks. Plus, use ground cinnamon to garnish.
  • Rice: You’ll need a cup of uncooked white rice. Short grain rice will yield a thicker and creamier final dish.
  • Milks: This creamy arroz con leche recipe calls for three types of milk: whole milk, evaporated milk, and sweetened condensed milk.

Tip: You can add raisins (stir them in with the milks), if you like.

How Do You Make Arroz con Leche?

Here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make arroz con leche:

  1. Bring the water and cinnamon stick to a boil, then simmer
  2. Add the rice, bring to a boil, and simmer until the water is absorbed.
  3. Stir in the milk, evaporated milk, and sweetened condensed milk.
  4. Garnish with cinnamon for serving.

How to Store Arroz con Leche

Allow the arroz con leche to cool, then store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to five days.

Ingredients
  1. 2 cups water
  2. 2 (3-inch long) cinnamon sticks
  3. 1 cup uncooked white rice
  4. 1 cup whole milk
  5. 1 (12-ounce can) evaporated milk
  6. 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
  7. 1 teaspoon cinnamon for garnish

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 515
  • Fat:14g
  • Carbs:84g
  • Protein:15g

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine water and cinnamon sticks in a medium saucepan over high heat.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until cinnamon releases its color and flavor, about 5 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    5. Add rice and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water is absorbed, about 20 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    6. Stir in whole milk, evaporated milk, and sweetened condensed milk. Cook uncovered over medium heat until mixture has thickened to desired consistency, 15 to 20 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    7. Serve warm or cold. Sprinkle each portion with ground cinnamon.
