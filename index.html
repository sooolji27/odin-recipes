Welcome to our website dedicated to the rich and diverse Cuban cuisine! Here, you will explore a variety of dishes that are the heart and soul of Cuban cooking. From the national dish, Ropa Vieja, to traditional sweets like Arroz con Leche, and emblematic drinks like the Cuba Libre, you will find an authentic representation of Cuban cuisine.

"Ropa Vieja", a dish that dates back to the Middle Ages in Spanish cuisine, is one of the most popular and loved dishes in Cuba. This dish is called "ropa vieja" in Spanish, which means "old clothes", and it is said to have originated when a poor man, with nothing more than his "old clothes", cut and cooked them. As he cooked the clothes, his love for his family turned them into meat, and thus was born this delicious dish.

As for sweets, "Arroz con Leche" is a popular dessert not only in Cuba, but in many countries in Latin America. The Cuban version of this rice pudding often includes ingredients like lemon peel or coconut grated.

Lastly, but no less important, the drinks. "The Cuba Libre", which translates to "free cuba" in English, is an emblematic drink of Cuba. This cocktail is composed of rum and lemonade, and is known for its refreshing taste and vibrant color.

Through our website, we want to share with you the rich history and delicious authenticity of Cuban cuisine. Whether you are looking for recipes, information about specific dishes, or simply want to learn more about the rich culinary culture of Cuba, we are here to help.
